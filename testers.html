ChronoSpark Tester Access

This page is for internal, closed, and future public testers helping validate ChronoSpark before release.

Getting Access

  1. Give the developer the Google account email you use for Play Store testing.
  2. Wait until your email is added to the tester list.
  3. Open the testing link from the same Google account.
  4. Install the app and report issues through the support/contact page.

What to Test

  • Sign-in and onboarding.
  • Task creation, editing, and completion.
  • SoulMaps, values, timelines, and milestones.
  • Offline or limited-mode behavior.
  • Crashes, freezes, layout issues, and broken screens.

Known Early-Access Notes

Some services may be unavailable while Supabase, Firebase, Play billing, cloud sync, and OAuth redirects are still being validated.
