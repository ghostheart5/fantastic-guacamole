ChronoSpark Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026. This policy explains how ChronoSpark handles information used for planning, account access, app reliability, and product improvement.

1. Information We Collect

ChronoSpark may collect account details, authentication identifiers, app settings, task and planning content you choose to save, device diagnostics, crash reports, and basic usage events.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide account sign-in and session management.
  • Save planning data, preferences, milestones, and progress.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • Analyze feature usage and diagnose errors.

3. Third-Party Services

ChronoSpark may use Supabase for authentication and data services, Firebase for analytics/crash reporting, and Google Play services for Android distribution and subscription handling.

4. Sensitive Data and Secrets

Do not submit passwords, financial information, medical records, or other highly sensitive secrets into general notes or planning fields unless a future feature explicitly supports protected storage.

5. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by visiting contact.html or emailing support@chronospark.app. Deletion requests are reviewed and processed according to applicable platform and legal requirements.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact ChronoSpark support at support@chronospark.app or through the support page.
