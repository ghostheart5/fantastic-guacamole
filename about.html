About ChronoSpark

A second brain for becoming who you are building toward.

ChronoSpark was created to bring planning, time, values, learning, and momentum into one focused system.

Why it exists

Life planning can feel scattered across notes, calendars, habit apps, journals, and unfinished goals. ChronoSpark connects those layers into a system that helps users choose the next meaningful action.

🌠

The Vision

ChronoSpark aims to become a personal operating system for growth: tasks, memories, goals, identity, milestones, and SI-powered planning in one evolving app.
