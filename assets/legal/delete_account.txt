Delete Account - ChronoSpark

Last updated: 2026-07-08

To request account deletion, email support@chronospark.app from or with the
email address connected to your ChronoSpark account.

How to request deletion:
- Send an email to support@chronospark.app.
- Use a subject such as "Account Deletion Request".
- Include the email address connected to your account so we can verify ownership.

What will be deleted:
- Your account profile and associated account data, including cloud-linked app
  data tied to that account.

What may be retained:
- Limited data may be retained when necessary for legal obligations, security
  monitoring, fraud prevention, tax and accounting requirements, dispute
  resolution, or other compliance duties.

Processing time and important notes:
- Verified account deletion requests are completed within 30 days.
- Deleting the app does not automatically delete your account.
- Account deletion is permanent and may not be recoverable.
