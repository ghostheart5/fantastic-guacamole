ChronoSpark Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18

ChronoSpark provides second-brain planning, synthetic intelligence insights, and premium features. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account data (email) for authentication.
  • App content such as tasks, notes, reflections, and settings you create.
  • Purchase status required to unlock premium access.
  • Operational diagnostics used to improve app reliability.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core app functionality and cross-session continuity.
  • Authenticate users securely with Firebase Authentication.
  • Deliver optional AI-generated responses based on your prompts.
  • Manage premium access and restore purchases.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Service providers may process data only as needed to deliver app features (for example: Firebase services and app store billing services).

Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active or as required to deliver services, enforce legal rights, and resolve disputes.

Your Rights

  • Request account data access or deletion.
  • Reset credentials and update account settings.
  • Request support via the contact method published in your store listing.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@chronospark.app
