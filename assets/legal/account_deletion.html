ChronoSpark Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-06-22

Use this page to request permanent deletion of your ChronoSpark account and the data associated with it.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email privacy@chronospark.app from the email address linked to your ChronoSpark account.
  2. Use the subject line Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address tied to your Firebase Authentication account if it differs from the sending address.

How We Verify the Request

We verify deletion requests by matching the request email to the authenticated account email and may ask you to confirm account ownership before deletion is completed.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account record.
  • Your account email and profile identifiers stored for sign-in.
  • ChronoSpark content associated with your account, including tasks, notes, reflections, and settings.
  • Premium access records that are linked only to your account profile.

What May Be Retained

Limited transaction, security, and legal-compliance records may be retained for a reasonable period when required to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or comply with applicable law.

Deletion Timeline

Verified deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. Any retained records are kept only for the minimum period required for legal or operational reasons.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account cannot be restored after deletion is completed.
  • You will lose access to saved app content tied to the deleted account.
  • Subscriptions are managed by your app store account and must be canceled through Google Play or the App Store if applicable.
