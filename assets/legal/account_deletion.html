How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your ChronoSpark account and associated data, send an email to privacy@chronospark.app with the subject line Account Deletion Request. Please include the email address linked to your account so we can locate and remove your records.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your account profile and authentication credentials.
  • All app content you created: tasks, notes, reflections, and settings.
  • Stored personal information associated with your account.

What Data May Be Retained

  • Billing or transaction records required by applicable law or app-store policies.
  • Anonymized operational diagnostics that cannot be linked back to you.
  • Information needed to resolve outstanding disputes or enforce legal rights.

Retention Period

Your account and personal data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your verified request. Any data retained for legal or billing compliance will be kept only for as long as required by law, and then permanently deleted.

Contact

For account deletion requests or privacy questions, contact: privacy@chronospark.app
