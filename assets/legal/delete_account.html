Delete Account - ChronoSpark

Last updated: July 7, 2026

To request account deletion, email support@chronospark.app from or with the email address connected to your ChronoSpark account.

How to Request Deletion

  • Send an email to support@chronospark.app.
  • Use a subject such as "Account Deletion Request".
  • Include the email address connected to your account so we can verify ownership.

What Will Be Deleted

After verification, we will delete your account profile and associated account data, including cloud-linked app data tied to that account.

What May Be Retained

Limited data may be retained when necessary for legal obligations, security monitoring, fraud prevention, tax and accounting requirements, dispute resolution, or other compliance duties.

Processing Time and Important Notes

  • Verified account deletion requests are completed within 30 days.
  • Deleting the app does not automatically delete your account.
  • Account deletion is permanent and may not be recoverable.
