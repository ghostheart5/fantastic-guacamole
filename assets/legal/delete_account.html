ChronoSpark Account Deletion Request

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Full account deletion request URL: https://ghostheart5.github.io/fantastic-guacamole/delete-account/

How to request account deletion

  1. Email support@chronospark.app.
  2. Use subject: Account Deletion Request - ChronoSpark.
  3. Include the account email (or sign-in provider email) used in ChronoSpark.
  4. Requests are acknowledged within 7 days and completed within 30 days.

Data deleted and retention

  • Deleted: account profile and associated app data tied to the account.
  • Temporary retention: backups and abuse-prevention logs may be retained for up to 30 days.
  • Legal retention: data required for legal, tax, fraud, or security obligations may be retained as required by law.
