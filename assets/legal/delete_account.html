ChronoSpark Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-07-08

To request permanent deletion of your ChronoSpark account and all associated data, follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email support@chronospark.app with the subject: Account Deletion Request – ChronoSpark.
  2. Include the email address associated with your ChronoSpark account.
  3. Requests are acknowledged within 7 days and completed within 30 days.

Data Deleted, Kept, and Retention

  • Deleted: account profile, in-app planning/task data, and cloud-synced app content linked to the account.
  • May be retained for up to 30 days: backup copies needed for disaster recovery and abuse-prevention logs.
  • May be retained when required by law: records needed for legal, tax, fraud, or security obligations.

Contact

Use the developer support contact listed in the app store listing, or email support@chronospark.app directly.
