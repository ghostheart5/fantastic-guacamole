ChronoSpark Account Deletion

This page explains how ChronoSpark users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

Account deletion requests are completed within 30 days.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your ChronoSpark account, email our support team at:

support@chronospark.app

Please include the following information in your email so we can identify your account:

  • The email address associated with your ChronoSpark account.
  • Your display name or username, if applicable.
  • A clear statement that you want your account deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, ChronoSpark will delete or anonymize account data associated with your user profile, including where applicable:

  • Account profile information.
  • App preferences connected to your account.
  • Saved planning data connected to your account.
  • Progress, goal, or productivity records connected to your account.
  • Cloud-stored user content associated with your account, if any.

What Data May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained when necessary for legitimate business, legal, security, fraud-prevention, tax, dispute-resolution, or compliance purposes.

Retained information is only kept for as long as necessary and is not used to recreate your deleted account.

Deletion Timeline

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.

If additional verification is required to protect your account from unauthorized deletion, we may contact you using the email address provided in your request.

Important Notes

Account deletion is permanent. Once your account and associated data are deleted, they may not be recoverable.

Deleting the ChronoSpark app from your device does not automatically delete your account. You must submit an account deletion request using the instructions on this page.

Contact

For deletion requests or privacy questions, contact:

support@chronospark.app
