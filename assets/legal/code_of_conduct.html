ChronoSpark Code of Conduct

Last updated: 2026-07-08

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors and users.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful and constructive in communication.
  • Assume positive intent and collaborate in good faith.
  • Provide useful feedback focused on improving the product.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
  • Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.
  • Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

Reporting

Report conduct concerns to support@chronospark.app.
