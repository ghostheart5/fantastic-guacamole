ChronoSpark Support & Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Closed testing builds may include QA-only access overrides and may not reflect final billing or account-management behavior.

Delete Account and Associated Data

Direct account deletion page: https://ghostheart5.github.io/fantastic-guacamole/delete-account/

  1. Email the developer support contact listed on the Google Play ChronoSpark listing.
  2. Use subject: Account Deletion Request - ChronoSpark.
  3. Include the account email (or sign-in provider email) used in ChronoSpark.
  4. Requests are acknowledged within 7 days and completed within 30 days.

Data Deleted, Kept, and Retention

  • Deleted: account profile, in-app planning/task data, and cloud-synced app content linked to the account.
  • May be retained for up to 30 days: backup copies needed for disaster recovery and abuse-prevention logs.
  • May be retained when required by law: records needed for legal, tax, fraud, or security obligations.

When Requesting Help

  • Include your device model and OS version.
  • Describe the screen or feature where the issue occurred.
  • State whether you used tester access or a real account.
  • Include reproduction steps or screenshots when possible.

Contact

Use the developer support contact listed in the app store listing.
