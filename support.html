Support Center

How can we help?

Use this page for login issues, tester access, sync questions, account deletion requests, bug reports, and general ChronoSpark support.

🔐

Login Issues

If sign-in fails, check your internet connection, confirm your email/provider account, and try again. If OAuth redirects fail, contact support with the device, browser, and screenshot.

☁️

Sync Problems

If cloud sync is unavailable, the app may start in limited mode. Local features may continue while backend services are being configured or restored.

🧪

Tester Access

For Google Play testing, make sure the email you use on Google Play is added to the correct tester group or testing list.

🗑️

Account Deletion

To request deletion, send your account email and request type through the contact page. Do not include passwords or secret keys.

Contact: Use contact.html for support, privacy, deletion, and tester requests.
