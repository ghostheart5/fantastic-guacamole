ChronoSpark Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

This policy explains what information ChronoSpark processes during normal use and closed community testing.

1. Information processed

  • Account information, such as an email address and display name, when authentication is enabled.
  • Content you create, including tasks, goals, notes, reflections, memories, settings, and prompts.
  • Productivity information, including focus sessions, completion history, energy, emotion, momentum, streak, and progression data.
  • Purchase or entitlement status when billing features are enabled.
  • Technical information, including app events, crash diagnostics, operating system information, an app-generated device identifier, and performance data.

2. Local storage

Most productivity content is stored locally on device storage, preferences, Hive databases, or secure storage where appropriate. Local information remains on the device until you remove it with a reset flow, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

3. External services

Depending on build configuration, ChronoSpark may use:

  • Firebase Analytics for aggregate usage events.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reports and technical diagnostics.
  • Supabase for authentication and aggregated daily productivity metrics.
  • A configured AI proxy and its upstream AI provider to generate conversational responses.
  • Google Play or Apple services for purchases and entitlement restoration when billing is enabled.

4. How information is used

  • Provide task, focus, progression, notification, insight, and coaching features.
  • Maintain continuity across app sessions.
  • Generate and improve recommendations and conversational responses.
  • Authenticate accounts and restore enabled purchases.
  • Diagnose crashes, measure reliability, and understand aggregate feature usage.

5. Sharing and sale

ChronoSpark does not sell personal information. Information is disclosed to configured service providers only as needed to support authentication, analytics, crash reporting, AI processing, billing, or other enabled app functionality.

6. Retention and deletion

Local data is retained until it is cleared from the device or the app is uninstalled. External providers may retain information according to their own operational and legal requirements. For account or external-data deletion requests, use the account deletion page linked in app settings.

7. Security

ChronoSpark uses platform storage and secure-storage facilities where appropriate. No system can guarantee absolute security, so avoid entering highly sensitive personal, medical, financial, or confidential information into tasks, reflections, or AI conversations.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated as testing and external-service configuration evolve. The effective date above identifies the current version.
