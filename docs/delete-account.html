ChronoSpark Account Deletion

Last updated: July 8, 2026

To request permanent deletion of your ChronoSpark account and associated cloud data, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email ghostheart131517@gmail.com with the subject: Account Deletion Request – ChronoSpark.
  2. Send the request from, or include, the email address associated with your ChronoSpark account.
  3. Requests are acknowledged within 7 days and completed within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Account profile data associated with your account identity.
  • Cloud-synced app data linked to that account.

What may be retained

  • Backup copies and abuse-prevention logs for up to 30 days.
  • Records retained when required by law (for example legal, tax, fraud, or security obligations).
